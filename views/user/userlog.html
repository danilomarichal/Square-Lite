What is project management software?

Project management software helps you plan, organize, and manage projects from beginning to end. Good project management software also acts as a collaboration tool that simplifies communication, makes it easy to share goals, coordinate work, give feedback, and provides a complete view of work so people tackle the right tasks at the right time.
